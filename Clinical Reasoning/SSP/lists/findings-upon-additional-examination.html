3.2. Findings upon additional examination

  • abnormal blood gas values
  • abnormal cardiac enzymes
  • abnormal coagulation profile
  • abnormal X-rays of abdomen, chest and skeleton
  • abnormal leukocyte count
  • abnormal ECG
  • abnormal electrolytes
  • abnormal faecal analyses, occult blood, parasites
  • abnormal glycaemia and markers of glycaemia homeostasis
  • abnormal histology, cytology and molecular genetic test
  • abnormal liver enzymes
  • abnormal markers of kidney function
  • abnormal serum lipids
  • abnormal thyroid hormones
  • abnormal urine sediment
  • anaemia
  • blood group compatibility
  • effusion detected by ultrasound (abdomen, pleura)
  • elevated biomarkers of inflammation
  • low bone density
  • nutritional deficiencies
  • polycythaemia
  • proteinemia, albuminemia
  • proteinuria
  • thrombopenia, thrombocytosis

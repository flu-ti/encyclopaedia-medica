3.1. Findings upon physical examination

  • abnormal blood pressure
  • abnormal external genitalia (female, male)
  • abnormal findings upon auscultation
  • abnormal findings upon inspection
  • abnormal findings upon palpation
  • abnormal findings upon percussion
  • bradycardia, tachycardia, irregular pulse
  • cachexia and malnutrition
  • cognitive impairment
  • cyanosis
  • disorganised speech
  • exophthalmos (proptosis)
  • halitosis (fetor oris)
  • gangrene
  • impairment or loss of consciousness, coma
  • oedema
  • pallor
  • pulseless patient
  • transient loss of consciousness, syncope

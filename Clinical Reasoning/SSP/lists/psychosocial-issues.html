4.7. Psychosocial issues

  • absenteeism (school, work)
  • concern about appearance, body image
  • domestic violence, sexual abuse, rape
  • harassing, bullying, mobbing
  • issues regarding sexual orientation
  • issues related to family life such as divorce, single parent and reconstructed family
  • loss, death, grieving process, illness of someone close
  • mental or spiritual suffering
  • problems related to work conditions, burnout, unemployment, financial problems

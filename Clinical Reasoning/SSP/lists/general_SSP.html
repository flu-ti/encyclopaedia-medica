1. General Complaints and Symptoms

  • abnormal perspiration
  • enlarged lymph nodes (lymphadenopathy)
  • excessive thirst, excessive fluid intake (polydipsia)
  • fatigue, tiredness
  • feeling of illness
  • fever, chills, hyperthermia
  • flushing
  • hypothermia
  • itching
  • pain of all types
  • sleep problems
  • swelling, oedema (diffuse or local)
  • unnatural death
  • unexpected or sudden death
  • weight gain, obesity
  • weight loss, malnutrition, loss of appetite

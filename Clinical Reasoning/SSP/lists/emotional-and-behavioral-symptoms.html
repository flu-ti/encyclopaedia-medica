2.7. Emotional and behavioral symptoms

  • anxiety and panic
  • attention deficit
  • change in behaviour
  • change in eating behaviour
  • change in mood
  • hyperactivity
  • irrational fear, fear of illness
  • Irritability, aggressive and violent behaviour
  • mental confusion, delirium
  • obsessive and/or compulsive behaviour
  • reactions to major stressful events
  • self-harm, including suicide
  • non-medical substance use (“misuse”), addiction e.g. tobacco, alcohol, illegal substances (“controlled medicines”), gambling and gaming
